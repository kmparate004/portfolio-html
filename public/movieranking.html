The Best Movies According To Kamakshi

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

TRAP

A father and his teen daughter attend a pop concert only to realize they've entered the center of a dark and sinister event.

MANJUMMEL BOYS

A group of friends get into a daring rescue mission to save their friend from Guna Caves, a perilously deep pit from where nobody has ever been brought back.

PARASITE

Greed and class discrimination threaten the newly-formed symbiotic relationship between the wealthy Park family and the destitute Kim clan.

EXHUMA

The process of excavating an ominous grave unleashes dreadful consequences buried underneath.
